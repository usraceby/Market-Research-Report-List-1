<p><strong>The Acrylic Glass Sheets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extruded Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Cast Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylic glass sheets market primarily consists of two types: extruded acrylic sheets and cast acrylic sheets. Extruded acrylic sheets are produced through a continuous process, resulting in a uniform thickness and lower production costs, ideal for applications requiring standard sizes and shapes. In contrast, cast acrylic sheets are made by pouring acrylic resin into molds, allowing for superior optical clarity, surface finish, and customization. These sheets are preferred for high-end applications, such as display cases and signage, due to their aesthetic appeal and durability.</p></p>
