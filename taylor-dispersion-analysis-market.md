<p><strong>The Taylor Dispersion Analysis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Taylor Discrete Model</li><li>Taylor Continuous Model</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Taylor Dispersion Analysis encompasses two market types: the Taylor Discrete Model and the Taylor Continuous Model. The Discrete Model focuses on analyzing the dispersion of particles or solutes in a series of discrete events or time intervals, providing insights into diffusion processes in batch systems. In contrast, the Continuous Model examines the steady-state dispersion in a continuous flow, offering a more dynamic perspective for analyzing transport phenomena in processes like chromatography and microfluidics. Both models aid in understanding how substances move in various media.</p></p>
