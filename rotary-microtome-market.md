<p><p>The rotary microtome market can be classified into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual microtomes require the operator to manually adjust the blade and thickness settings, offering precision but demanding more skill and effort. Semi-automatic microtomes provide some automated features, such as controlled slicing, while still requiring operator intervention. Fully automatic microtomes streamline the slicing process with advanced automation, facilitating higher throughput and consistency, making them ideal for high-volume laboratories and research applications.</p></p>
