<p><p>The weather forecast system market comprises three key types: Satellite-based systems, Ground-based systems, and Airborne systems. Satellite-based systems utilize orbiting satellites to gather atmospheric data over large areas, offering global coverage and remote sensing capabilities. Ground-based systems rely on terrestrial weather stations and radar networks to monitor local conditions with high accuracy. Airborne systems involve aircraft equipped with meteorological instruments, collecting real-time data from various altitudes, enhancing short-term forecasting and relational data for specific regions.</p></p>
