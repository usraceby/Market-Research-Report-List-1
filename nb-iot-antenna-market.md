<p><p>The NB-IoT antenna market is primarily divided into two types: built-in and external antennas. Built-in antennas are integrated directly into devices, offering a compact solution ideal for space-constrained applications. They provide convenience but may have limited performance compared to external options. In contrast, external antennas are standalone units that can be mounted separately, delivering enhanced range and signal strength, making them suitable for demanding environments. Both types cater to various IoT use cases, balancing design and performance needs.</p></p>
