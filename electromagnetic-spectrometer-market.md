<p><p>The electromagnetic spectrometer market comprises two primary types: atomic spectrometers and molecular spectrometers. Atomic spectrometers analyze elements by measuring the interaction of light with atoms, typically used for identifying and quantifying metals and trace elements. In contrast, molecular spectrometers focus on molecules, assessing their vibrations and rotations to understand chemical structures and compositions. Both types play crucial roles in research, environmental monitoring, and quality control across various industries, providing essential insights into material properties and concentrations.</p></p>
