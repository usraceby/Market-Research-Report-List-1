<p><p>The Food and Beverage Checkweigher market includes two primary types: Belt-Based Checkweighers and Roller Conveyor Based Checkweighers. Belt-Based Checkweighers utilize a continuous belt to transport products for weight measurement, ensuring accuracy in high-speed production environments. In contrast, Roller Conveyor Based Checkweighers leverage rollers to facilitate the movement of items, providing a stable and efficient way to handle heavier products. Both types are essential for quality control, minimizing product giveaway, and complying with regulatory standards in the food and beverage industry.</p></p>
