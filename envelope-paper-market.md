<p><p>The envelope paper market is divided into two primary types: hard paper and soft paper. Hard paper is thicker, providing sturdiness and durability, making it ideal for official documents and important correspondence. It often features a refined finish, enhancing its professional appearance. Soft paper, on the other hand, is lighter and more flexible, suitable for casual letters and promotional materials. It offers versatility in design but lacks the rigidity of hard paper. Both types cater to distinct consumer needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
