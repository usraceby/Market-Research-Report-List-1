<p><p>The Mask Review Equipment market features two primary methods: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). The DD method involves comparing individual semiconductor dies to identify defects or irregularities, ensuring quality control during manufacturing. Meanwhile, the DB method leverages a database of previously analyzed dies for comparison, facilitating quicker defect detection by referencing established benchmarks. Both methods are crucial in enhancing semiconductor fabrication quality, optimizing production efficiency, and reducing costs in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
