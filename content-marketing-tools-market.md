<p><p>Content marketing tools are categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based tools are hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access them via the internet. They offer flexibility, scalability, and ease of collaboration, making them ideal for teams spread across different locations. In contrast, on-premises tools are installed locally on an organization’s servers, providing greater control over data security and customization. While cloud-based solutions often require a subscription, on-premises tools typically involve a one-time purchase and maintenance costs.</p></p>
