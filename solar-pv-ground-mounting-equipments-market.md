<p><p>The solar PV ground mounting equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: crystalline silicon systems and thin film systems. Crystalline silicon systems, known for their high efficiency and longevity, utilize silicon wafers to convert sunlight into electricity, making them the most prevalent choice in the market. In contrast, thin film systems use layers of photovoltaic material, offering flexibility and lightweight options, which are advantageous for specific applications and environments. Both systems play crucial roles in advancing solar energy deployment.</p></p>
