<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Clad Plate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Explosion Bonded Clad Plate</li><li>Roll Bonded Clad Plate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The clad plate market encompasses two primary types: explosion bonded clad plates and roll bonded clad plates. Explosion bonded clad plates involve a high-energy detonation process that fuses two different metal layers, resulting in excellent strength and corrosion resistance. In contrast, roll bonded clad plates are created through a mechanical rolling process, ensuring a strong bond between layers while being more cost-effective for mass production. Both types cater to various industries, including oil and gas, chemical, and marine, where enhanced material performance is crucial.</p></p>
