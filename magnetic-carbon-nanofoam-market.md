Granules are small, irregularly shaped particles offering enhanced permeability and improved flow properties, making them suitable for filtration and adsorption. Powders consist of fine particles with high surface-to-volume ratios, providing versatility in composite materials and energy storage solutions. Each type caters to specific applications and industry needs.</p></p>
