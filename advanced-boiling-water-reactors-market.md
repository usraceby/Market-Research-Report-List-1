<p><p>Advanced Boiling Water Reactors (ABWRs) primarily feature two market types based on steam generation cycles: Single Cycle Steam Generation and Dual Cycle Steam Generation. </p><p>In Single Cycle Steam Generation, the reactor produces steam in a single pass, providing direct and efficient power conversion. Conversely, Dual Cycle Steam Generation utilizes both high-pressure steam and secondary systems, allowing for improved thermal efficiency and flexibility in energy output. Each type addresses specific energy demands and operational efficiencies within the nuclear power sector.</p></p>
