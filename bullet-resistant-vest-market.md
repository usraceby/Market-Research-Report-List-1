<p><p>The bullet-resistant vest market is segmented into two main types: soft armor and hard armor. Soft armor is typically made from flexible materials such as Kevlar or aramid fibers, providing comfort and mobility while offering protection against lower-caliber firearms. In contrast, hard armor incorporates rigid plates made from materials like ceramic or polyethylene, designed to withstand higher-caliber rounds and offer greater protection. Both types cater to different needs, with soft armor being favored for everyday use and hard armor for tactical situations.</p></p>
