<p><p>Fiber Bragg Gratings (FBGs) are optical devices used in fiber optic communication and sensing applications. <p><ul><li>Uniform Fiber Bragg Grating</li><li>Non Uniform Fiber Bragg Grating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fiber Bragg Gratings (FBG) market comprises two main types: Uniform Fiber Bragg Gratings and Non-Uniform Fiber Bragg Gratings. Uniform FBGs feature a consistent refractive index modulation along the fiber length, allowing precise wavelength selection for applications like sensing and communications. In contrast, Non-Uniform FBGs exhibit variable modulation, enabling enhanced performance in applications requiring specific filtering or multi-wavelength support. These distinctions cater to diverse sectors, including telecommunications, aerospace, and structural health monitoring, driving innovations across the industry.</p></p>
