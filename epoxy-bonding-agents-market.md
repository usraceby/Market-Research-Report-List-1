<p><p>The epoxy bonding agents market encompasses three main viscosity types: low, medium, and high. Low viscosity agents are fluid and penetrate surfaces easily, ideal for applications requiring deep adhesion and quick curing. Medium viscosity agents balance flow and thickness, suitable for general bonding and repairs. High viscosity agents are thicker and provide gap-filling properties, making them perfect for structural applications where strength is paramount. Each type serves specific needs in construction, automotive, and industrial sectors, enhancing performance and durability.</p></p>
