<p><strong>The Wireless Fire Detection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Addressable Wireless Fire Detection Systems</li><li>Conventional Wireless Fire Detection Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wireless Fire Detection Systems market is primarily categorized into two types: Addressable Wireless Fire Detection Systems and Conventional Wireless Fire Detection Systems. Addressable systems allow for the identification of individual detectors, providing precise location information during an alarm, which aids in efficient response. In contrast, Conventional systems operate on a simpler framework, grouping multiple detectors on a single circuit, making them generally more affordable but less sophisticated in pinpointing specific alarm sources. Each type serves distinct needs based on complexity and budget.</p></p>
