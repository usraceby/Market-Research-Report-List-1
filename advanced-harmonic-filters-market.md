<p><p>The advanced harmonic filters market is categorized into three main types: passive filters, active filters, and hybrid filters. Passive filters utilize passive components like inductors and capacitors to mitigate harmonics, offering cost-effectiveness but limited flexibility. Active filters employ power electronics to dynamically counteract harmonics, providing greater adaptability and efficiency. Hybrid filters combine both passive and active elements, striking a balance between performance and cost, making them suitable for various industrial applications while enhancing overall power quality and system reliability.</p></p>
