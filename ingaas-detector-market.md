<p><p>The InGaAs detector market comprises two primary types: multi InGaAs photodiode arrays and single InGaAs photodiodes. Multi InGaAs photodiode arrays consist of multiple detectors integrated into a single unit, enabling enhanced sensitivity and improved spatial resolution for applications like imaging and spectroscopy. In contrast, single InGaAs photodiodes are standalone devices that offer high responsiveness for specific tasks such as telecommunications and high-speed data transfer. Both types cater to diverse applications across sectors like defense, telecommunications, and industrial monitoring.</p></p>
