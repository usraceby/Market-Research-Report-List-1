<p><strong>The High Speed Industrial Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Line Scan Camera</li><li>Area Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-speed industrial cameras market comprises primarily two types: line scan cameras and area scan cameras. Line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, making them ideal for applications in continuous processes, such as conveyor systems and high-speed production lines. They excel in capturing detailed images of long objects. In contrast, area scan cameras capture complete frames at once, suitable for various applications including inspection, quality control, and robotics, accommodating broader field views and stationary objects.</p></p>
