<p><p>CNC rotary tables are categorized into three main types: vertical, horizontal, and tilting. Vertical rotary tables feature a flat work surface aligned vertically, ideal for jobs requiring precise positioning. Horizontal rotary tables allow for wide workpieces to be machined on a flat, horizontal surface, facilitating ease of access and stability. Tilting rotary tables combine both vertical and horizontal capabilities, enabling angled machining for complex part geometries. Each type serves specific applications, enhancing efficiency and versatility in CNC machining processes.</p></p>
