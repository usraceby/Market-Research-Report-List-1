<p><ul><li>Spiral Path Deduster</li><li>Elevating Path Deduster</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Tablet Dedusters market features two main types: Spiral Path Dedusters and Elevating Path Dedusters. Spiral Path Dedusters utilize a helical design to effectively remove excess dust from tablets, ensuring smooth surfaces and enhancing product quality. Conversely, Elevating Path Dedusters use a vertical lifting mechanism, guiding tablets through a series of screens to eliminate dust and contaminants while maintaining optimal production efficiency. Both types aim to enhance tablet integrity and ensure compliance with industry standards.</p></p>
