<p><p>The power transmission belts market includes various types: </p><p>1. **Synchronous Belts**: These belts have teeth that engage with sprockets, providing precise timing for motion transmission, commonly used in robotics and automotive applications. </p><p>2. **V-Belts**: Characterized by their wedge shape, V-belts are widely used for their flexibility and efficiency in transferring power in machinery and industrial settings. </p><p>3. **Round Polyurethane Belts**: These belts offer durability and resistance to abrasion, ideal for light-duty applications in conveyors and material handling. </p><p>4. **Others**: This category encompasses specialty belts like flat belts and timing belts, catering to niche markets and specific mechanical requirements.</p></p>
