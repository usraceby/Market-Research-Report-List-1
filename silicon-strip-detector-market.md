<p><strong>The Silicon Strip Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Sided</li><li>Double-Slided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicon Strip Detectors are classified into two main types: Single-Sided and Double-Sided detectors. Single-Sided detectors feature sensors that only collect charge on one side, making them simpler and cost-effective for applications requiring lower resolution and less complexity. In contrast, Double-Sided detectors have sensors on both sides, allowing for improved spatial resolution and better position measurement, ideal for advanced particle physics experiments and high-energy applications. The choice between them depends on the specific requirements of the detection process.</p></p>
