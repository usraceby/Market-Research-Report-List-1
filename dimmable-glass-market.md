<p><ul><li>PDLC Glass</li><li>Electrochromic Glass</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dimmable glass market comprises several types, primarily including PDLC (Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal) glass and electrochromic glass. PDLC glass uses liquid crystals suspended in a polymer matrix, allowing for quick transitions between opaque and transparent states, ideal for privacy. Electrochromic glass, on the other hand, changes transparency through an electric charge, offering energy efficiency and glare control. Other market types may include suspended particle devices and liquid crystal displays, each with unique applications in architecture and automotive sectors.</p></p>
