<p><p>Bird control hazing systems are divided into two primary market types: spray type and vapor type. Spray type systems utilize aerosolized solutions to create scents or tastes that deter birds from specific areas. This method is often localized and effective in targeting specific nesting or feeding spots. Vapor type systems, on the other hand, release gaseous deterrents over a broader area, effectively dispersing unwanted birds from large spaces. Both methods aim to minimize bird-related challenges in various environments.</p></p>
