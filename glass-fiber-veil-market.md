<p><strong>The Glass Fiber Veil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet-Laid Process</li><li>Dry-Laid Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glass Fiber Veil market features two primary types based on manufacturing processes: Wet-Laid and Dry-Laid. The Wet-Laid process involves suspending glass fibers in a liquid medium, allowing for uniform distribution and stronger bonding, making it ideal for applications requiring enhanced durability and low weight. In contrast, the Dry-Laid process utilizes a dry web of fibers, often resulting in lower production costs and faster processing times, suitable for applications where flexibility and ease of handling are prioritized.</p></p>
