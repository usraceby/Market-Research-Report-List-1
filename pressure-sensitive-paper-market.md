<p><p>The pressure-sensitive paper market is categorized into various types based on thickness and application. LLW (Light Light Weight) caters to ultra-light applications, and LLLW (Super Light Light Weight) is meant for specialized uses. Finally, 4LW (Four Light Weight) provides options for extremely thin, flexible needs across various industries.</p></p>
