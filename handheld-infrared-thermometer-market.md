<p><p>The handheld infrared thermometer market primarily features two types: forehead type and ear type thermometers. Forehead type thermometers measure surface temperature from the forehead, providing quick and non-invasive results, making them ideal for use in homes and clinical settings. Ear type thermometers use infrared technology to measure temperature inside the ear canal, offering accuracy and speed. Both types cater to diverse needs, including home healthcare, hospitals, and schools, emphasizing efficiency in monitoring body temperature for fever detection.</p></p>
