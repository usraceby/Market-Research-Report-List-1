<p><strong>The Carbon Fiber Rod Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PAN-Based Carbon Fiber Rod</li><li>Pitch-Based Carbon Fiber Rod</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon fiber rod market is primarily categorized into two types: PAN-based carbon fiber rods and pitch-based carbon fiber rods. PAN-based carbon fibers are derived from polyacrylonitrile, known for their high strength-to-weight ratio and versatility in various applications, including aerospace and automotive industries. In contrast, pitch-based carbon fibers, produced from coal tar or petroleum pitch, offer superior thermal and electrical conductivity, making them suitable for specialized applications such as electronics and high-performance sports equipment. Both types cater to distinct market needs.</p></p>
