<p><ul><li>Mineral Admixture</li><li>Chemical Admixture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cement admixtures market is divided into two main types: mineral admixtures and chemical admixtures. Mineral admixtures include materials like fly ash, silica fume, and slag that enhance concrete performance through improved durability and workability. Chemical admixtures are additives such as superplasticizers, retarders, and accelerators that modify the properties of fresh and hardened concrete, facilitating various construction needs like quicker setting times or enhanced flowability. Both types contribute to the sustainability and efficiency of concrete production.</p></p>
