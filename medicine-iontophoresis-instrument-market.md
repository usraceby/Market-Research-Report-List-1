<p><p>The medicine iontophoresis instrument market is categorized into three types based on frequency: high frequency, intermediate frequency, and low frequency. High-frequency iontophoresis instruments utilize rapid electrical impulses, enhancing the penetration of medications for effective treatment. Intermediate-frequency devices balance speed and penetration, suitable for various therapeutic applications. Each type offers distinct advantages and serves different therapeutic needs, driving innovation and demand within the healthcare sector.</p></p>
