<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Ultrasonic Testing Machine</li><li>Phased Array Ultrasonic Testing Machine</li><li>TOFD Ultrasonic Testing Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic tester market encompasses various types of inspection technologies. Conventional ultrasonic testing machines are widely used for basic flaw detection and material thickness measurements, employing a single probe for sound wave transmission. Phased array ultrasonic testing machines utilize multiple transducer elements to create adjustable beams, allowing for enhanced inspection capabilities and defect characterization. Time-of-Flight Diffraction (TOFD) ultrasonic testing machines are specialized for detecting and sizing flaws, offering high sensitivity and precision by measuring the time it takes for sound waves to travel.</p></p>
