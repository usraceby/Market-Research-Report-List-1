<p><p>The electrical contacts market is categorized into three main types based on voltage levels. Low-voltage electrical contacts are utilized in applications with less than 1,000 volts, typically in consumer electronics and lighting. Medium voltage electrical contacts operate between 1,000 to 35,000 volts, commonly found in industrial and utility sectors. High-voltage electrical contacts exceed 35,000 volts, used in power generation and transmission. Light load electrical contacts are designed for low current applications, ideal for relays and switches that manage minimal electrical loads.</p></p>
