<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Fans and Blowers</li><li>Axial Fans and Blowers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial fans and blowers market is categorized into two main types: centrifugal fans and blowers, and axial fans and blowers. Centrifugal fans utilize a rotor to accelerate air, creating high pressure and suitable for ventilation and exhaust applications. In contrast, axial fans move air along the axis of the fan blade, providing high airflow at lower pressure, ideal for cooling and drying. Both types serve critical functions across industries, including HVAC, manufacturing, and agricultural sectors.</p></p>
