<p><p>The Continuous Non-invasive Glucose Monitoring System (CNGMS) market is categorized into two main types: wearable CNGMS and implantable CNGMS. Wearable CNGMS includes devices like smartwatches or patches that display glucose levels in real-time, offering convenience and ease of use. In contrast, implantable CNGMS involves devices surgically placed under the skin, providing continuous monitoring with long-term data accuracy. Both types aim to enhance diabetes management by reducing the need for traditional finger-prick testing and improving patient compliance.</p></p>
