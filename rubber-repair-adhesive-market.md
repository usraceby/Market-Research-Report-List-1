<p><strong>The Rubber Repair Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Bond</li><li>Cold Bond</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber repair adhesive market is divided into two primary types: hot bond and cold bond adhesives. Hot bond adhesives require heat to cure and create a strong, permanent bond, making them suitable for demanding applications like tire repairs. Cold bond adhesives, on the other hand, cure at room temperature and are ideal for quick repairs or applications where heat cannot be applied. Both types cater to different repair scenarios, offering flexibility in various industrial and consumer contexts.</p></p>
