<p><p>The Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) system market encompasses various techniques for coating materials. Cathodic Arc Vapor Deposition uses plasma to create films, while Electron Beam Vapor Deposition employs high-energy electrons to vaporize a target material. Sputter Deposition involves ejecting atoms from a target to deposit on a substrate. Ion Implantation introduces ions to alter material properties. Pulsed Laser Vapor Deposition uses focused laser pulses to vaporize a target, forming thin films. Each method is tailored for specific applications in electronics, optics, and protective coatings.</p></p>
