<p><p>Radio Frequency over Glass (RFoG) is a technology that uses glass fiber to transmit radio frequency signals, primarily for broadband communications such as internet and television services. The RFoG technology enhances service delivery in fiber-to-the-home (FTTH) applications, making it an attractive option for telecommunications providers.</p><p>The Radio Frequency over Glass Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.2% during the forecast period.
