<p><p>The heavy-duty electric vehicle batteries market primarily consists of two types: lead-acid batteries and lithium-ion batteries. Lead-acid batteries are traditional, cost-effective solutions known for their reliability and ease of recycling, though they have lower energy density and shorter lifespan. In contrast, lithium-ion batteries offer higher energy density, longer life cycles, and faster charging capabilities, making them preferable for modern heavy-duty electric vehicles. The choice between these battery types often depends on performance requirements, cost considerations, and environmental impact.</p></p>
