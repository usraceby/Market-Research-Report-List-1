<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The IoT Pressure Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Pressure Sensor</li><li>Gauge Pressure Sensor</li><li>Vacuum Pressure Sensor</li><li>Differential Pressure Sensor</li><li>Sealed Pressure Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT pressure sensors market is segmented into various types based on measurement capabilities. Absolute pressure sensors measure pressure relative to a vacuum, providing precise readings. Gauge pressure sensors measure pressure relative to atmospheric pressure and are commonly used in industrial applications. Vacuum pressure sensors detect low pressures in a vacuum system, while differential pressure sensors measure the difference between two pressure points, useful for flow measurement. Sealed pressure sensors offer a stable reference between absolute and gauge measurements, ensuring reliable data in varying conditions.</p></p>
