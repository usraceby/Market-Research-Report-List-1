AC Type recorders, on the other hand, employ alternating current, which can be more suitable for certain diagnostic applications due to its sensitivity to electrical noise. </p><p>DC & AC Type recorders combine both methods, offering enhanced versatility in monitoring heart rhythms, thereby catering to diverse patient needs and clinical scenarios.</p></p>
