<p><strong>The Audio Monitoring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Audio Monitoring</li><li>Passive Audio Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audio monitoring market can be divided into two main types: active audio monitoring and passive audio monitoring. Active audio monitoring involves real-time analysis and feedback on audio signals, allowing for immediate adjustments and enhancements to sound quality. This type is commonly used in live sound environments and broadcasting. In contrast, passive audio monitoring focuses on recording and reviewing audio without real-time interventions. It is often utilized in post-production processes where sound quality is evaluated after the fact.</p></p>
