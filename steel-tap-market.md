<p><p>The steel tap market encompasses two primary types: pocket tapes and surveyors tapes. In contrast, surveyors tapes are longer, durable tapes intended for precise measurements in land surveying and engineering applications. Both types are essential for accurate measurement, with pocket tapes favoring ease of use and portability, while surveyors tapes prioritize length and precision for professional surveying tasks.</p></p>
