<p><strong>The Limited Slip Differential (LSD) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical LSD</li><li>Electronic LSD</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A Limited Slip Differential (LSD) improves vehicle traction by distributing power between wheels. Mechanical LSD uses gears and clutches to restrict wheel spin, enhancing performance during cornering. Electronic LSD employs sensors and software to control braking and torque distribution, adapting to driving conditions. Other market types may include viscous LSDs, which utilize fluid to manage torque transfer, and torque vectoring LSDs that actively direct power to the wheel with the most grip, further optimizing handling and stability.</p></p>
