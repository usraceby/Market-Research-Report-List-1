<p><p>The Nickel Foam market comprises two primary types: Continuous Nickel Foam and Special Nickel Foam. Continuous Nickel Foam is produced through a seamless process, offering uniform pore structures essential for applications like batteries and fuel cells, characterized by high conductivity and structural integrity. Conversely, Special Nickel Foam is tailored for specific applications, featuring distinct properties such as enhanced porosity or surface treatment. This type caters to specialized industries, including aerospace and medical devices, where unique performance characteristics are required.</p></p>
