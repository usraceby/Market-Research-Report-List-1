<p><p>The seed treatment materials market is categorized into two main types: powder form and liquid form. Powder form treatments involve dry formulations that can be dusted or coated onto seeds, providing ease of application and effective adherence. Liquid form treatments offer a more uniform coverage and can penetrate seeds better, often facilitating easier incorporation of active ingredients. Both forms are designed to protect seeds from pests, diseases, and environmental stresses, ultimately enhancing germination and crop yield.</p></p>
