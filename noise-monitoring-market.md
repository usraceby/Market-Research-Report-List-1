<p><p>Noise monitoring encompasses three primary market types: short-term, long-term, and permanent monitoring. Short-term monitoring involves brief assessments typically used for specific events or situations, providing immediate insights into noise levels. Long-term monitoring focuses on extended evaluations over weeks or months, capturing variations and trends in noise pollution. Permanent monitoring systems are installed for continuous assessment, providing real-time data and alerts, essential for ongoing compliance and urban planning. Each type serves specific needs in managing and mitigating noise pollution effectively.</p></p>
