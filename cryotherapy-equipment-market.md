<p><p>The cryotherapy equipment market is divided into two main types: Whole-Body Cryotherapy and Local Cryotherapy. Whole-Body Cryotherapy involves exposing the entire body to extremely cold temperatures, typically in a specialized chamber, to promote recovery, reduce inflammation, and enhance overall wellness. Local Cryotherapy, on the other hand, targets specific areas of the body using localized cold applications, like ice packs or cryo devices, to alleviate pain, treat injuries, and reduce muscle soreness. Both types offer therapeutic benefits tailored to individual needs.</p></p>
