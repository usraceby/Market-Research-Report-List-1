<p><ul><li>Variable Chamber Baler</li><li>Fixed Chamber Baler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The square hay baler market is primarily divided into two types: variable chamber balers and fixed chamber balers. Variable chamber balers can adjust the size of bales during operation, allowing for greater flexibility in bale dimensions and density. This versatility is beneficial for various types of crops and moisture levels. In contrast, fixed chamber balers produce bales of a consistent size and weight, offering simplicity and ease of use, making them suitable for users seeking reliable and straightforward performance.</p></p>
