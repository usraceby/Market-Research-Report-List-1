<p><p>The mobile robots in logistics market primarily includes Automated Guided Vehicles (AGVs) and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs). AGVs operate on predefined paths, using magnets or sensors for navigation, making them suitable for repetitive tasks in warehouses. In contrast, AMRs leverage advanced technologies like computer vision and AI to navigate dynamically through environments, allowing for greater flexibility and efficiency. Both types enhance operational efficiency by automating material handling, reducing labor costs, and improving safety within logistics operations.</p></p>
