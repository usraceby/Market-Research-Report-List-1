<p><p>The biochemical reagent market encompasses various analytical techniques used for separating, identifying, and quantifying biomolecules. Chromatography involves the separation of compounds based on their movement through a stationary phase, allowing the analysis of complex mixtures. Mass spectrometry is used to determine the mass-to-charge ratio of ions, enabling precise molecular identification. Electrophoresis separates molecules based on their size and charge, facilitating the study of proteins and nucleic acids. Together, these methods drive advancements in research, diagnostics, and therapeutics.</p></p>
