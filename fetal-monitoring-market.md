<p><ul><li>Antepartum Fetal Monitoring</li><li>Intrapartum Fetal Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fetal monitoring market is divided into two primary types: antepartum and intrapartum fetal monitoring. Antepartum fetal monitoring includes techniques used to assess fetal health before labor, often utilizing non-stress tests and biophysical profiles. Intrapartum fetal monitoring, on the other hand, occurs during labor and focuses on real-time assessment of the fetus's condition through methods like electronic fetal monitoring. Both types aim to ensure the safety of the mother and baby, guiding clinical decisions during pregnancy and childbirth.</p></p>
