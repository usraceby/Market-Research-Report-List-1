<p><strong>The Aluminium Conductor Steel-reinforced Cable (ACSR) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ACSR – Aluminum Conductor Steel-Reinforced</li><li>ACSR/AW – Aluminum Conductor Aluminum-Clad Steel-Reinforced</li><li>ACSR/TW – Trapezoidal Aluminum Conductor Steel-Reinforced</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aluminium Conductor Steel-reinforced (ACSR) cable market consists of three main types. ACSR combines aluminum conductors with a steel core for strength and conductivity. ACSR/AW features aluminum-clad steel for enhanced corrosion resistance while maintaining strength. ACSR/TW utilizes trapezoidal-shaped aluminum conductors for improved conductivity and reduced wind loading. Each type caters to specific requirements in power transmission and distribution, balancing conductivity, mechanical strength, and environmental resilience for diverse electrical applications.</p></p>
