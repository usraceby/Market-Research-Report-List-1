<p><ul><li>Absorbable Suture Wire</li><li>Non-Absorbable Suture Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The suture wire market is divided into two main types: absorbable and non-absorbable suture wires. Absorbable suture wires are designed to degrade over time within the body, eliminating the need for removal and often used in internal tissues and organs. Non-absorbable suture wires, on the other hand, are made from materials that remain intact, providing long-term support and are typically used in skin, tendon, and various surgical procedures. Both types play critical roles in wound closure and surgical repair.</p></p>
