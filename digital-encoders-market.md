<p><p>The digital encoders market is primarily segmented into two types: optical and magnetic. Optical encoders use light to detect position changes and are known for high precision and resolution, making them ideal for applications requiring accuracy. In contrast, magnetic encoders rely on magnetic fields to determine position, offering robustness and resistance to environmental factors like dust and moisture. Both types are essential in various industries, including robotics, automation, and automotive, catering to different operational requirements and conditions.</p></p>
