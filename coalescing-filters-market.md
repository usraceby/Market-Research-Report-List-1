<p><p>Coalescing filters are essential for removing water and particulates from liquid fuels and gases. The market is divided into vertical and horizontal coalescing filters. Vertical coalescing filters are designed for space efficiency and effective separation, making them suitable for applications where height is limited. In contrast, horizontal coalescing filters offer high dirt-holding capacity and are often used for larger flow applications. Both types aim to enhance filtration efficiency and protect downstream equipment by ensuring clean fuel and reducing maintenance costs.</p></p>
