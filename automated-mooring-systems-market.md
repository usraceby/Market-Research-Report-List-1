<p><ul><li>Mooring Lines</li><li>No Mooring Lines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated Mooring Systems are categorized into two main market types: mooring lines and no mooring lines. The mooring lines market involves traditional systems that utilize physical lines to secure vessels to docking structures, offering reliability and stability. In contrast, the no mooring lines market leverages advanced technologies such as vacuum or magnetic systems for securing ships without the need for physical lines, promoting efficiency and reducing maintenance. Both systems aim to enhance operational safety and streamline vessel handling processes in port environments.</p></p>
