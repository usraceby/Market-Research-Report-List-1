<p><p>The Wire Bond Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI) market is segmented into 2D and 3D inspection types. 2D AOI primarily focuses on surface analysis, capturing images from a top-down perspective to identify defects in wire bonds, such as misalignments and voids. In contrast, 3D AOI incorporates depth perception and volumetric data, allowing for a more comprehensive assessment of wire bond integrity, including height and shape variations. Each type serves distinct quality control needs in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
