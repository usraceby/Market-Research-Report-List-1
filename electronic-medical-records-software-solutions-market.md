<p><p>The Electronic Medical Records (EMR) software solutions market is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based EMR systems are hosted on remote servers, allowing easy access and scalability, while reducing local IT infrastructure costs. In contrast, on-premise EMR solutions are installed locally on healthcare facilities' servers, offering greater control over data security and customization but requiring higher upfront investment and maintenance. Both types serve to streamline patient data management and enhance healthcare delivery efficiency.</p></p>
