<p><p>The automotive millimeter wave radar IC market features three primary types: GaAs, SiGe BiCMOS, and RF CMOS. GaAs (Gallium Arsenide) offers high efficiency and sensitivity, making it suitable for advanced radar systems. SiGe BiCMOS (Silicon-Germanium Bipolar CMOS) combines the benefits of silicon and germanium, enabling compact and cost-effective designs with good performance. RF CMOS (Radio Frequency Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) is also popular due to its scalability and integration capabilities, providing a balanced solution for automotive radar applications.</p></p>
